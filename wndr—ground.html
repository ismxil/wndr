Visual Artists in 2021
$0
Paid to artists

In the US, visual artists receive zero royalties on secondary market sales. When their work appreciates in value, the profit goes entirely to the collector and gallery.

II
Music Royalties in 2021
$2.7b
Paid to songwriters and composers

In 2021 alone, ASCAP distributed $1.25 billion, BMI distributed $1.33 billion, and SESAC distributed hundreds of millions in royalties to songwriters, composers, and music publishers.

"The model relies on artists living in poverty initially, while collectors reap the benefits of their success years later."
Sarah Park
"If a piece of art appreciates in value from $5k to $50k, the artist should share in that growth. It's their intellectual property."
David Chen
"We need a system that supports artists long-term. Royalties are standard in music and publishing; visual arts must catch up."
Elena Rodriguez
